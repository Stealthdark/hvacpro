About HVACPro

HAVCPRO AIR is a leading air conditioning supply and installation company in Victoria, delivering complete climate control solutions across all major brands—from reliable budget systems to premium, high-performance units.

We are Victoria’s No.1 dealer for Carrier and Toshiba and a trusted industry name for quality and reliability. Our work is carried out by fully licensed installers, with Panasonic Australia–accredited technicians, ensuring every installation meets strict manufacturer and industry standards.

From product supply to professional installation, HAVCPRO AIR provides end-to-end air conditioning solutions for residential and commercial projects—designed for efficiency, compliance, and long-term performance.
